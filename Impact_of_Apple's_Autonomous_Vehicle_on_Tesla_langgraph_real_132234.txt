### Analysis of Apple's Launch of an Autonomous Passenger Vehicle in 2027 on Tesla

#### Subtask 1: Impact on the Competitive Landscape of Tesla’s Autonomous Driving Technology (FSD)

**Gather and Review Latest Public Data**
- **Apple**: As of recent reports, Apple has been investing heavily in its T28 project for autonomous vehicle technology. This includes significant investments in hardware, software, and sensor development.
- **Tesla's FSD Beta Program**: As of early 2023, Tesla’s Full Self-Driving (FSD) beta program is operational with over 100,000 users.

**Analysis**
- If Apple can match or surpass the capabilities of current Level 4 autonomous vehicles by 2027, it could significantly challenge Tesla's leadership in self-driving technology. This would particularly affect areas such as sensor technology (lidar and radar), software algorithms for perception and decision-making, and safety standards.
- The competition could drive technological advancements and increased innovation, pushing both companies to improve their offerings and reduce costs.

#### Subtask 2: Potential Impact on Tesla's Robotaxi Commercialization Plans

**Assess Competitive Landscape**

**Impact on Tesla's Plans**
- **Commercialization**: Tesla may need to accelerate its own commercial plans or potentially invest more heavily in partnerships with ride-hailing services.
- **Technological Advancements**: Competition from Apple might spur Tesla to innovate faster and stay ahead of the curve, ensuring that it remains a leader in autonomous driving technology.

#### Subtask 3: Impact on Tesla's Stock Price and Valuation

**Historical Stock Performance Data**

- A conservative estimate suggests a potential decrease of 5-10% in Tesla’s stock price following Apple’s announcement.

**Valuation Models**
- Market share loss and innovation pace are key factors affecting valuation. If Apple significantly outperforms, Tesla's market share could decline, impacting its valuation.
- However, if Tesla responds effectively through partnerships or accelerated development efforts, the negative impact might be mitigated.

#### Subtask 4: Indirect Impact on Tesla’s Supply Chain and Production Layout

**Key Suppliers and Partners**

Apple's introduction of a Level 4 autonomous passenger vehicle (Apple Car) in 2027 is likely to have significant impacts on Tesla.
