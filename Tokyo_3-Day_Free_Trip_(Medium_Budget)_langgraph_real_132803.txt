### Day 1: Shinjuku - Shibuya

**Morning:**
- **Attractions**: Start your day at [Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden](https://www.shinjukugyoen.or.jp/en/), a beautiful park with Japanese, English, and French gardens.
- **Transportation**: Walk or take the subway (about 5 minutes to Shibuya).
- **Lunch**:
  - **Meal Suggestions**: Visit [Shinjuku Station](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shinjuku_Station) for quick and affordable options like *okonomiyaki* (savory pancakes), *tonkatsu* (breaded deep-fried pork cutlet), or try the famous *shabu-shabu* at nearby restaurants.

**Afternoon:**
- **Attractions**: Visit the [Yoyogi Park](https://www.yoyogipark.jp/en/) and explore the bustling atmosphere of the [Yoyogi Park Koenji Area](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yoyogi_Park_Koenji_Area), known for its street art, cafes, and boutiques.
- **Transportation**: Walk or take a short taxi ride (about 15 minutes to Shibuya).
- **Dinner**:
  - **Meal Suggestions**: Enjoy *yakitori* (grilled chicken skewers) at [Shibuya Station](https://www.shinjukushi.net/kyoiku/shibuya_e/en/) or try the traditional *katsu sushi* in nearby bars.

### Day 2: Shibuya - Harajuku

**Morning:**
- **Attractions**: Visit the trendy neighborhood of [Harajuku](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Harajuku) to see the unique fashion and culture.
- **Transportation**: Take a train from Shibuya Station (about 15 minutes).
- **Lunch**:
  - **Meal Suggestions**: Try *monjayaki* (a type of pancake) at [Sakuragaoka Monja Street](https://www.sakuragaokamonjacho.com/) or enjoy some *katsu* sandwiches.

**Afternoon:**
- **Attractions**: Head to the famous [Meiji Shrine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Meiji_Shrine) and explore the serene grounds. You can then visit the adjacent forested park.
- **Transportation**: Take a train from Harajuku Station (about 10 minutes).
- **Dinner**:
  - **Meal Suggestions**: Have *donburi* (rice bowl) at [Shibuya Station](https://www.shinjukushi.net/kyoiku/shibuya_e/en/) or try *tempura* in nearby restaurants.

### Day 3: Shibuya - Tokyo Skytree

**Morning:**
- **Attractions**: Visit the iconic [Tokyo Skytree](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tokyo_Skytree) for panoramic views of the city.
- **Transportation**: Take a train from Shibuya Station (about 30 minutes).
- **Lunch**:
  - **Meal Suggestions**: Have *sushi* at the base of Tokyo Skytree or try some *gyoza* (pan-fried dumplings) nearby.

**Afternoon:**
- **Attractions**: Explore the surrounding areas such as [Asakusa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asakusa) and visit the [Sensoji Temple](https://www.senso-ji.or.jp/en/) or go to [Ueno Park](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ueno_Park).
- **Transportation**: Take a train from Asakusa Station (about 30 minutes back to Shibuya).

### Transportation Suggestions

**Prioritize subway/walking as much as possible.**
- **Subway Lines**: Use the JR Yamanote Line, Tokyo Metro Chiyoda Line, and Tokyo Metro Ginza Line for efficient travel.
- **Walking**: For short distances, walking is recommended to save money and enjoy the surroundings.
